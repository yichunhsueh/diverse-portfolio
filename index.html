About

More About Me.

Hi! You also can call me Winnie. I possess extensive experience in art design and management, encompassing graphic design, product development, marketing planning, web design, UI/UX, packaging design, and commercial photography.
I am actively developing my career as a hairstylist while maintaining my passion and professionalism in graphic design. Combining these two areas will allow me to reach higher levels of creativity and design, and enable me to provide more diverse services to my clients.

In recent years, my graphic design work has focused on management functions, continuously executing various design projects. This involves optimizing workflows, ensuring effective communication, enhancing team collaboration, and improving design capabilities and quality. Currently, I am also actively developing my hairstyling skills, applying my design experience to the hairdressing field to achieve a good synergy between graphic design and hairstyling, providing clients with creative and professional services.
